About Me

Not long ago, I was an architect. A building building architect, not the building web kind. And a construction project manager. Then a pandemic happened.

I just wanted to make a simple website. So I bought an online course from a youtube ad which promised me to teach how to make a website, among other things like millions of dollars and a fool of my clients. It also taught me to not bother with obsolete technologies such as html ,css and javascript. I did learn about making wordpress sites. Since an "inception" of divs inside divs was not to my taste, I also learned to bother.

Time-travel a few months forward, I found MDN and the world of online tutorials. Then one curiosity led to another and I learned HTML, CS, JS, React and was making some fun applications using it. I also discovered other technologies on the way. I tried designing with UI principles, I tried frameworks like Vue and Svelte, and CSS technologies like BEM, Sass, Less, Bootstrap, Tailwind, Bulma and styled components.

I still find it a little unbelievable that the whole apps can be written on the web.I have stumbled through the looking glass. This is fun.
